Magical Mapua Home

The outdoor and living areas of this home are lucky enough to enjoy a view of D'urville across the sea on a clear day, and equally as appealing, a serene pastoral view that you could never tire of. Walk out the front door and you can enjoy the scenic Waimea estuary before making your way down towards the very popular Mapua Village.

Built by GJ Gardner in 2016, 85 Mapua Drive is gifted on the inside as well and exudes comfort and a contemporary design; the spacious 195m2 layout will inspire and charm you from the minute you step foot in the door of the home. Designed to exude a sense of space with wider than average hallways, lavish bathrooms and separate laundry with stacks of storage, and plentiful light this home feels relaxed and easy to live in, with plenty of style. The bedrooms have a sense of privacy and separation leading off as they do from the a central hallway which conveys you to bright and sunny living area with open plan kitchen including stainless steel benchtops and scullery, relaxing lounge and decent sized dining incorporating a private and sunny indoor/outdoor area off the dining, and further out amongst the garden a lovely al fresco seating area perfectly positioned for friends and family to take advantage of the views.

All bedrooms are a good size with built in wardrobes, with the master having sliding doors to the private patio area, that all-important walk in robe and generous spa like ensuite with beautiful free standing bath and double vanity.

With double glazing, rib raft foundation, concrete flooring in the living, completely insulated and built to the sun, you will be just right, all year round. There is also an internal double garage. The tastefully landscaped grounds are lovely enough to leave as is or add more to, your choice. Whatever you choose the flat grounds are sure to be a hit with family and friends during warmer months where the sun sets in the back offering ample time for BBQs and a quiet G&T.
